Basic Rules

Class A - 1 Day Ban & 10 Warning Points.

Class B - 3 Day Ban & 15 Warning Points.

Class C - 7 Day Ban & 30 Warning Points.

Class D - Perm (Can apply for an unban after 30 Days) & 50 Warning Points.

100 Points = Remove whitelist.

Kol player maya3mel 7ata fail fi 1 month kemel (ki yabda actif) wala 2 months (mehouch actif) ysirlou reset Warning Points .
